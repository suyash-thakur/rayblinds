About our Company

Manufacturer to Customer Approach

Ray Blinds – based in Lower Mainland, BC – are the premier suppliers of only the best-designed ultramodern as well as classic blinds and shades in over 10+ major cities. We offer durable pocket-friendly quality products at your convenience. Our efficient customer service, installation, and production teams work round the clock to achieve your desired degree of privacy, ambiance, & lighting.

Trust us to design timeless, classy décor for your windows, made with top-shelf fabrics & machinery imported from leading manufacturers. Our shades & shutters include energy-efficient, mechanized, cordless alternatives in addition to multi-continental, luxurious, and traditional ones to suit your sense of aesthetic. Everything, at reasonably low prices – thanks to our direct manufacturer-to-customer approach. Our expertly stylized world-class blinds are the perfect fit and look for your home.
